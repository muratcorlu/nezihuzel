                                                                                                          9

                                                       Bir ömür oldu, senin **gül bahçeni** görmedik,

                                                       O baygın, o insanın **aklını başından** alan

                                                       **Nergis** gözlerini seyretmedik...

                                                       **Vefa gibi** halktan **gizlenmişsin.**                            

                                                       Nice zamandır ki **senin**

                                                       Güzel **yanaklarını** görmedik.

                                                                                                                         **Mevlânâ**
