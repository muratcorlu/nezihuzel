                                                                                                   Sana **çok** yakınız

                                                                    

                                                                     Ey **dost** ! dostlukta sana çok **yakınız**.

                                                                     Nereye ayak **basarsan** o yerin **toprağı** oluruz.

                                                                     Aşıklık **mezhebinde reva mıdır ki ?**

                                                                     **Alemi seninle görelim** de, seni görmeyelim.

                                                                                                                                                                           **Mevlânâ**
