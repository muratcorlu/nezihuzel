                                                                                                           6

                                                                                              

                                                     Bu **aşk ateşi** bizi pişirir,

                                                     Her gece **harabata** doğru çeker,**götürür.**

                                                     Başkası **bizi** bilmesin**,tanımasın** diye,

                                                     Yalnız **harabat** ehliyle buluşturur.

                                                     Onlarla beraber **otururuz.**

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          **Mevlânâ**
