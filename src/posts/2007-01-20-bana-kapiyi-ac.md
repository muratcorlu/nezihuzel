---
title: "Bana kapıyı aç"
date: "2007-01-20"
categories: 
  - Dervish
---

Benim **aşktan** başka

                                                                         Hiçbir **arkadaşım** yoktu      

                                                                         Ve olmadı.                                                  

                                                                         Ne **dünyaya** gelmeden **önce**,

                                                                         Ne de **sonra**

                                                                         **Aşksız** yaşamadım.

                                                                         **Canım** içimden bana **şöyle** diyor:

                                                                           -Ey aşk **yolunun olgun yolcusu**,

                                                                           Bana **kapıyı** aç.

                                                                                                                                                 **Mevlânâ**
